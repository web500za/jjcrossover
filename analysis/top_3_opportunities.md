### Summary Title
**Salesforce License Optimization & Contract Renegotiation**

### Current State

- Enterprise CRM contracts typically include 15-30% unused or underutilized licenses
- Multi-year contracts often lock in higher rates that can be renegotiated
- Duplicate functionality may exist with HubSpot ($32,187) which should be eliminated

### Recommended Actions
1. Conduct license utilization audit to identify inactive/underused seats
2. Consolidate to appropriate license tiers (Enterprise â†’ Professional where applicable)
3. Eliminate HubSpot overlap - migrate any unique functionality to Salesforce
4. Renegotiate contract with competitive pressure from Microsoft Dynamics
